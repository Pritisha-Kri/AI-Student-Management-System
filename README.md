# 🎓 AI-Student-Management-System

A modern and intelligent **Student Management System** built to manage school operations efficiently with **AI-assisted timetable generation, attendance analysis, performance tracking, student risk prediction, and role-based dashboards**.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The **AI-Student-Management-System** is a full-stack web application designed for schools and educational institutions. It provides separate interfaces for **Administrators, Teachers, and Students**, making academic and administrative work easier, faster, and more organized.

The system uses AI-assisted logic to support timetable generation, attendance monitoring, academic performance analysis, and student risk prediction.

---

## ✨ Key Highlights

- 🎯 Role-based dashboards for Admin, Teacher, and Student
- 🤖 AI-assisted timetable generation
- 📊 Attendance analysis and risk detection
- 📈 Student performance tracking
- 🧠 Weak subject and student risk prediction
- 🗓️ Class-wise and teacher-wise timetable management
- 📝 Exam, marks, and result management
- 📢 Notices and announcements
- 💳 Fee tracking and payment status
- 📄 Reports and analytics

## 👥 User Interfaces

### 🛡️ Administrator
- Manage students, teachers, classes, sections, and subjects
- Generate AI-assisted timetables
- Manage attendance, fees, exams, results, notices, and reports
- View AI dashboard and student risk insights

### 👩‍🏫 Teacher
- View assigned classes and subjects
- Mark student attendance
- Add homework and assignments
- Enter marks and view student performance
- View personal timetable and AI suggestions

### 🎒 Student
- View profile, attendance, timetable, homework, and notices
- View exam schedule, marks, and results
- Check fee status
- Receive AI-based study suggestions

---

## 🤖 AI-Assisted Features

### AI Timetable Generation
Generates optimized class-wise and teacher-wise timetables using:
- Teacher availability
- Subject frequency
- Period timing
- Subject difficulty level
- Workload balancing
- Clash prevention

### AI Attendance Analysis
Identifies students with:
- Low attendance
- Frequent absences
- Attendance risk level

### AI Performance Tracking
Analyzes:
- Weak subjects
- Strong subjects
- Academic progress
- Performance trends

### AI Student Risk Prediction
Combines:
- Attendance percentage
- Exam marks
- Homework submission status

To classify students as:
- Low Risk
- Medium Risk
- High Risk
